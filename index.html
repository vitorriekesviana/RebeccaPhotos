01 The Approach

Every photograph is a small act of journalism — a record of what was true in a particular light, on a particular afternoon, between particular people. I bring that same care to the work, whether it's a first look before a wedding, a cap tossed at graduation, or a birthday table crowded with the people who matter most.

— Rebecca Kraft
